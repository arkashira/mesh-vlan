## Customer Segments
- Small to medium-sized businesses (SMBs) in Thailand needing advanced WiFi solutions
- Educational institutions requiring robust network management
- Hospitality industry (hotels, resorts) with multiple SSIDs for guests and staff
- Home users with large properties or challenging signal environments

## Value Propositions
- Simplified UI for easy management of VLANs and multiple SSIDs
- Mesh networking to overcome signal loss in large or complex environments
- Cost-effective solution compared to enterprise-grade alternatives
- Localized support and customization for Thai market

## Channels
