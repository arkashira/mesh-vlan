# Customer Journey Map

## Aware
- **Trigger Event**: User experiences poor WiFi coverage or struggles with managing multiple networks in a complex environment.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about advanced WiFi solutions, confusion about technical terms like VLAN and mesh networking.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, confusion, need for a better solution.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content, clear explanations of benefits, case studies.
- **Metric**: Number of unique visitors to educational content.

## Consider
- **Trigger Event**: User researches solutions for better WiFi management and coverage.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelming number of options, difficulty comparing features, lack of clear pricing.
- **User Emotions**: Overwhelmed, cautious, hopeful.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Comparative guides, transparent pricing, demo videos.
- **Metric**: Time spent on product comparison pages.

## Try
- **Trigger Event**: User decides to test the mesh-vlan solution.
- **Friction Points**: Complex setup process, lack of trial period, technical support availability.
- **User Emotions**: Excited, anxious, hopeful.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Easy setup guide, 24/7 customer support, free trial period.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from trial sign-ups to paid users.

## Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: User successfully sets up and uses the mesh-vlan solution.
- **Friction Points**: Initial learning curve, integration with existing infrastructure, ongoing maintenance.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfied, relieved, confident.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Personalized onboarding, regular updates, community support.
- **Metric**: Customer satisfaction score (CSAT).

## Expand
- **Trigger Event**: User experiences the benefits and considers additional features or scaling.
- **Friction Points**: Cost of additional features, complexity of scaling, lack of advanced support.
- **User Emotions**: Confident, ambitious, eager.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Upsell opportunities, advanced training, dedicated account manager.
- **Metric**: Number of additional features purchased or upgrades made.
