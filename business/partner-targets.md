# Partner Targets
## Overview
The mesh-vlan product requires strategic partnerships to enhance its features, expand its reach, and drive revenue growth. The following partner targets are identified based on their relevance to the product's capabilities and market demand.

## Partner Target List
### 1. OpenWRT
* SaaS/API: OpenWRT API
* Integration Effort: M
* Value-Add: Simplifies mesh networking configuration for users
* Free-Tier Limits: 10 devices
* Affiliate/Revenue-Share: Yes (OpenWRT offers a revenue-sharing program)
* Rationale: OpenWRT is a popular open-source firmware for routers and access points, which aligns with mesh-vlan's mesh networking capabilities.

### 2. Unifi
* SaaS/API: Unifi API
* Integration Effort: M
* Value-Add: Enhances SSID-to-VLAN mapping and multiple SSID support
* Free-Tier Limits: 5 devices
* Affiliate/Revenue-Share: Yes (Ubiquiti, Unifi's parent company, offers a revenue-sharing program)
* Rationale: Unifi is a widely used network management platform that integrates with various access points, making it an ideal partner for mesh-vlan.

### 3. Cisco Meraki
